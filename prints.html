Portrait of Wonder Bread No. 1, 2 and 3. 2014, limited edition of 50 signed prints of Nelson’s 1977 Carrara Statuario Marble sculptures, HOLOS/Series 7, No.s 1, 2 and 3.

The Portrait of Wonder Bread print series, signed and dated by Jud Nelson, is a juxtaposition of the dispassionate study of bread with the imprint of the artist’s hand on each piece of bread. Nelson collaborated with Townhouse.bz to offer Portraits of Wonder Bread 1, 2 and 3. A series of limited edition photograph prints of Nelson’s Carrara Statuario Marble sculptures HOLOS/Series 7, 1977. Coolly minimalist with deep, matte blacks and greys on premium, matte, archival paper, the prints highlight the breathtaking detail of Nelson’s sculptures.
